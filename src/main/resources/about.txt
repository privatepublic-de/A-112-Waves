<p>
	<b>About A-112 Waves</b><br>
	Version 1.0 by Peter Witzel, Jan 2015
</p>
<p>
	With this programm you can generate and upload waves and wavetables to the <a href="http://www.doepfer.de/a112.htm">Doepfer
 	A-112</a> modular system sampler. It can generate waveforms with a simple script or resample existing WAV files.
 </p>
<p>
	Visit <a href="http://www.privatepublic.de/software/a112">www.privatepublic.de/software/a112</a> 
	for help, documentation and contact.<br>
	This is absolutely free software, project source on <a href='https://github.com/'>GitHub</a>.
</p>
